Server Invites


GROW YOUR PLAYERBASE AND GIVE PLAYERS REWARDS FOR INVITING THEIR FRIENDS WITH THIS SIMPLE SKRIPT!

Info:

Server Invites is a simple skript that allows you to give your players rewards for inviting their friends onto their server! You can customize the rewards players receive for inviting someone and being invited, the max amount of players someone can invite, and more!

This skript is currently created and developed by me, Tresre. If you have any questions, feel free to join my support discord and ask.


Setup:

By default, this skript requires no setup and works right away. However, it does offer customization options with a config inside the skript for customizing how many people someone can invite and what rewards are given to the person who sends the invite and the person who receives the invite. The config is located at the top of the skript, and I recommend you don't change any code below the config section. Please keep in mind that this skript does need at least 1 plugin slot open to install skript if it isn't already installed.


Required addons:
-None


-In Game Commands:

The skript has some in-game commands that you can use to control the skript! The commands are as follows:
-/invite - Invite someone to the server.
-/serverinvites - For OPs, allows you to see who someone has invited as well as reset all invites.


The 5 built-in features are as follows:

-Reward players for inviting someone.
-Give players a reward for being invited.
-View who anyone invited with an op-only command.
-Reset all invites on the server with an op-only command.
-Set the max number of invites in the built-in config.
